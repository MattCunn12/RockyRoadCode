Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about our tractors, equipment, and services.

What types of tractors do you offer?

We offer a wide range of tractors, from compact models for small farms to heavy-duty tractors for large-scale operations. Some of our most popular brands include John Deere, Kubota, and New Holland.

Do you provide maintenance services for tractors?

Yes, we offer maintenance and repair services for all types of tractors. Our skilled technicians can help with oil changes, tire repairs, and troubleshooting common issues.

How can I purchase a tractor from Rock Road Tractor Supply?

You can purchase a tractor directly from our store or through our website. We also offer financing options to help make the purchase more affordable.

What should I do if my tractor is not working properly?

If your tractor is having issues, we recommend checking the basic components like the oil, fuel, and tire pressure. If the problem persists, please contact our service team for a professional inspection.

Do you offer any warranty on your tractors?

Yes, we offer warranties on most of the tractors we sell. The warranty typically covers parts and labor for a specified period. Please ask our sales team for more details on specific models.
